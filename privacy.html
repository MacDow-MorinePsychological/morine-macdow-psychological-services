Privacy Policy

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all clients and website visitors.

Collection of Information

Personal information submitted through this website (such as name and email address) is used solely for the purpose of responding to inquiries regarding services.

Confidentiality

Psychological services are provided in accordance with the ethical standards and confidentiality requirements set by the Nova Scotia regulatory body for psychologists. Information shared within therapy is kept confidential except where disclosure is required or permitted by law.

Electronic Communication

While reasonable steps are taken to protect information submitted through this website, electronic communication (including email and contact forms) may not be fully secure. Sensitive clinical information should not be submitted through this form.

Website Analytics

This website may use basic analytics tools to understand visitor traffic. No identifying personal health information is collected through analytics.

Contact

Questions regarding privacy practices may be directed through the contact form on the main page.
