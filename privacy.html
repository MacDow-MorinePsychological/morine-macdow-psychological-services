Privacy Policy

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of individuals who visit this website.

Collection of Information

Personal information submitted through this website (such as name and email address) is used solely for the purpose of responding to inquiries regarding services.

Confidentiality

Psychological services are provided in accordance with the ethical standards and confidentiality requirements established by the Nova Scotia Regulator of Psychology (formerly the Nova Scotia Board of Examiners in Psychology) and the Canadian Code of Ethics for Psychologists.

Electronic Communication

While reasonable steps are taken to protect information submitted through this website, electronic communication (including email) may not be fully secure. Sensitive personal or health information should not be included in initial messages.

Website Analytics

Basic website analytics may be used to understand general website usage. This information does not identify individual visitors.
