Child & Youth-Focused Care

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services is dedicated exclusively to the psychological care of children and youth. Services are structured to reflect developmental stage, emotional capacity, and family context.

Areas of Support

  • Anxiety and worry
  • Managing strong emotions
  • Behavioural challenges
  • School stress and school avoidance
  • Low mood and depression
  • Confidence and self-esteem
  • Peer relationships and social challenges
  • Family changes and transitions
  • Grief and loss
  • Body image and disordered eating patterns
  • Sleep difficulties
  • Stress and coping skills
  • Personal growth and well-being

Clinical Approach

Services integrate Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), attachment-informed principles, and mindfulness-based strategies. Interventions are evidence-based and thoughtfully adapted to each child’s individual strengths and needs.
