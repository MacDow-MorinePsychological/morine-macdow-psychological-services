Child & Youth-Focused Care

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services is dedicated exclusively to the psychological care of children and youth. Services are virtual and structured to reflect developmental stage, emotional capacity, and family context.

Philosophy

Children enter the world curious, capable and full of potential. Each child brings unique strengths and ways of experiencing the world, along with a natural capacity to grow and adapt. At times, life circumstances, developmental differences, or emotional challenges can make those strengths harder to see or access.

Therapy offers a space to slow down, reflect, and better understand those experiences. Through compassionate listening, collaboration, and evidence-based guidance, young people can build on their existing strengths, develop new skills, and move toward greater confidence and well-being.

At Morine-MacDow Psychological Services, we work collaboratively with children, adolescents, and families to understand each person’s experiences and to support meaningful, lasting change.

  • Managing strong emotions

Therapeutic Approaches

At Morine-MacDow Psychological Services, support is tailored to each young person’s strengths, needs, and developmental stage.

An integrative approach is used, drawing from a range of evidence-based therapies and strategies. This allows treatment to remain flexible and responsive to each child or adolescent’s unique experiences.

Approaches that may be utilized include:

  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy
  • Family-Based Treatment for Eating Disorders
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy
  • Emotion-Focused Therapy
  • Mindfulness-Based Strategies
  • Motivational Interviewing
  • Solution-Focused Therapy
  • Dialectical Behaviour Therapy
  • Narrative Therapy
  • Play-Based Approaches
