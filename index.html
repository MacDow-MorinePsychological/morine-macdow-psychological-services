Child & Youth-Focused Care

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services is dedicated exclusively to the psychological care of children and youth. Services are structured to reflect developmental stage, emotional capacity, and family context.

Parents are often active partners in the therapeutic process, especially for younger children, which ensures that support extends beyond sessions and into everyday life.

Services Offered

Child Therapy (Ages 5–12)

Support for anxiety, emotional regulation challenges, behavioural concerns, and school-related stress.

Adolescent Therapy (Ages 13–17)

Guidance through identity development, peer relationships, mood concerns, and transitional periods.

Young Adult Therapy (Ages 18-22)

Assistance with post-secondary education plans and changes that arise with increased autonomy and independence.

Parent Consultation

Collaborative planning and practical strategies to strengthen confidence, consistency, and connection at home.

Virtual Psychological Services

Secure online sessions available to youth and their families throughout Nova Scotia.

Clinical Approach

Services integrate various therapeutic approaches that are evidence-based and thoughtfully adapted to each youth's individual strengths and needs.

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services does not provide crisis services. If you or your child are concerned about safety issues or are experiencing an urgent mental health issue, please go to the nearest emergency department or call 911.
