Child & Youth-Focused Care

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services is dedicated exclusively to the psychological care of children and adolescents. Services are structured to reflect developmental stage, emotional capacity, and family context.

Parents are active partners in the therapeutic process, ensuring that support extends beyond sessions and into everyday life.

Services Offered

Child Therapy (Ages 6–12)

Support for anxiety, emotional regulation challenges, behavioural concerns, and school-related stress.

Adolescent Therapy (Ages 13–17)

Guidance through identity development, peer relationships, mood concerns, and transitional periods.

Parent Consultation

Collaborative planning and practical strategies to strengthen confidence, consistency, and connection at home.

Virtual Psychological Services

Secure online sessions available to families throughout Nova Scotia.

Clinical Approach

Services integrate Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), attachment-informed principles, and mindfulness-based strategies. Interventions are evidence-based and thoughtfully adapted to each child’s individual strengths and needs.

Emergency Information

Morine-MacDow Psychological Services does not provide crisis services. If your child is experiencing an urgent mental health emergency, please contact your nearest emergency department or call 911.
