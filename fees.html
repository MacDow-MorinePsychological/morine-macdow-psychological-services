Fees

Psychological services are provided virtually across Nova Scotia.

The fee for a standard clinical session is $225 per hour, in accordance with the recommended fee schedule of the Association of Psychologists of Nova Scotia.

Most extended health insurance plans provide coverage for services delivered by a Registered Psychologist. Clients are responsible for payment at the time of the appointment and may submit receipts to their insurance provider for reimbursement.

Payment methods and additional details will be discussed during the initial consultation.
