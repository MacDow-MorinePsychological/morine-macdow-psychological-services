Fees

The fee for a 50-minute psychotherapy session is $225, consistent with the recommended fee guidelines of the Association of Psychologists of Nova Scotia.

Services provided by a private psychologist are not covered under MSI. However, many extended health benefit plans provide reimbursement for services delivered by a Registered Psychologist. Clients are responsible for exploring their specific benefit coverage and for submitting receipts to their insurance provider for reimbursement.

Financial Accessibility

Access to mental health care is important. A limited number of reduced-fee appointments are available for individuals experiencing financial hardship. If financial concerns may prevent you from seeking support, please reach out to discuss available options.

Cancellation Policy

Appointments are reserved specifically for each client. If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please provide at least 24 hours notice. Providing advance notice helps ensure that appointment times can be offered to other clients who may be waiting for support. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice, or missed appointments, may be billed at the full session fee.

Payment methods and additional administrative details will be discussed during the initial consultation.
